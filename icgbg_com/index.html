Learn About Us

25 Years Experience

ICG is a global business company headquartered in Bulgaria, dedicated to providing global clients with comprehensive consulting services including strategic planning, business transformation, risk management, technology consulting and sustainable development. With branches in more than 10 countries around the world, it has more than 100 professional consultants, including strategic consultants, data scientists, technical experts and industry experts, who come from diverse cultural backgrounds and have extensive industry experience and expertise.

Since its establishment, the company has adhered to the core values of "customer first, innovation-driven", providing customized solutions for customers to help them solve complex business challenges, improve operational efficiency and achieve sustainable growth. The company's clients cover a wide range of industries including finance, manufacturing, retail, healthcare, energy, government, and nearly half of the Fortune 500 companies.

In terms of talent development, the company focuses on fostering diversity and inclusiveness among its employees, attracting and retaining top talent from around the world by offering professional training, career path planning and flexible work arrangements. The company also actively participates in social welfare activities, giving back to society through volunteer services, charitable donations, etc., demonstrating its sense of social responsibility.

Looking ahead, the company will continue to focus on global business trends, deepen cooperation with clients, use innovative technologies to drive industry progress, and strive to become one of the most respected business consulting firms in the world.
